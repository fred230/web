    The name Vineyard

    mistake or design?

    The Bible is full of examples that can be used to inspire us in our daily lives. One example comes from John 15:16 which states, “You did not choose me, but I chose you and appointed you so that you might go and bear fruit—fruit that will last.” This verse speaks to the importance of bearing lasting fruit through our actions, words, deeds and ministries, activities that can best take place in a Vineyard(garden) setup.

    • In Vineyard, you bear lasting fruit while also representing God's kingdom here on earth through faithful service.
    • In Vineyard, you live out your calling with dignity and purpose.
    • In Vineyard, you live as an emissary knowing that you are chosen by God as peculiar people extinguishing darkness and shewing forth praises in songs and in service to humanity.

    In short, Vineyards goal is to develop and empower vineyarders to become evangelists, mentors and talent appreciators in and out of campus.
